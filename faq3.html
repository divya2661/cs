1) How are the hostel facilities?

The current residential campus (temporary) on New Pali road is a self-sufficient, verdant campus where both faculty and students reside as one large family. There is a flat system 2BHK which accommodate 5 students and built in bathroom.

2) How about the food at hostel?

There are two separate mess, one in the residential area and the other one in the academic campus. The mess offers good quality food, regularly monitored by the wardens for hygiene and nutritional values, and at very affordable rates.

3) Is there medical aid available at the hostel?

IIT Jodhpur provides 24x7 health facilities to students at the residential campus. The institute also has tie-ups with some hospitals in the city to cater to the medical needs of the students.

4) What about the recreation facilities?

Every hostel consists of a recreation room T.V room where students can sit and enjoy matches, serials, movies along with few other activities like table tennis, carom etc. It is a place to chill and enjoy.

5) Are there sports facilities available at the hostel?

Hostel consists of a basketball court, volleyball court, badminton court and indoor sports. Other sports facilities are available at the institute.

6) Is there a gym for students?

A full-fledged, well equipped gymnasium is present in the residential campus which is open from 5:00 am to 10:00 pm. A student can avail all the facilities to stay healthy and to maintain his/her physique.

7) Does the campus have canteen facility?

There are 2 canteens, one at residential area and the other one in academic campus. It facilitates good fast food, fresh juices and various other snacks for the students.

8) What are the other student activities running in the campus?

There are various student run clubs active under student body namely Technical council (N.U.T.S), Cultural council (W.A.V.E.S), Arts and design (M.A.D), Sports council (S.A.G.E), Management council (P.R.O.M) and academic council (A.R.A). Besides these are social activities under Parivartan and many other activities and festivals which helps students to learn and enjoy their time at hostel.

9) Is there an ATM at the residential campus?

The residential area has a branch of SBI (State Bank of India) and also an ATM of SBI enabling students to make transactions with ease. There are several other banks namely UCO, HDFC, SBBJ, etc. close to the academic area.

10) The Institute and the Residential Campus are separated by a distance of 14 kms. How do we do daily travelling?

The Institute has buses plying between institute and hostels on regular basis. The students and also their parents can avail this facility. In case of extreme emergency, auto-rickshaw can also be taken.
