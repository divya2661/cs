Before leaving for IIT Jodhpur, please check that you have the following documents and things with you.


IDENTITY

1. Admit Card of the JEE, 2013

2. Letter of Qualification of JEE, 2013

4. Any Other valid identification (if you have one)

3. Letter of acceptance/ admission letter from IIT Jodhpur


DOCUMENTS

1. At least 8 recently taken passport size photographs

2. Cash receipts of all the payments made at the time of seat allotment

3. Class X pass certificate (Original and at least 02 photocopies,attested)

4. Class XII pass certificate (Original and at least 02 photocopies,attested)

5. Proof of family income that might be useful for applying for any scholarship

a) Income Tax Return

b) Form-16, for government employees

c) Affidavit

d) Caste Certificate

6. Relevant medical reports


OTHER ESSENTIAL THINGS

1. Track pants or short for compulsory physical training

2. A pair of sports shoes for compulsory physical training

3. Bed linen, toiletries and sanitary items, alarm clock etc.


THINGS THAT ARE USUALLY FORGOTTEN

1. Tooth Brush and Tooth Paste

2. Towels

3. Travel Tickets

4. Cell Phone Charger (if any)

5. AN OPEN MIND
