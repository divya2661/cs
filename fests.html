Ignus - Inter-College Techno-Cultural Fest

IGNUS is the inter college socio-techno-cultural fest of IIT Jodhpur. 3rd year of the fest saw a great and enthusiastic crowd from different parts of the country. The pronites added glory to the fest with around 6,000 footfalls in the pronites of AGNEE – The Band and RABBI SHERGILL – The Punjabi Singing Maestro in IGNUS’13. There were a large number of events in the fest which added the flavour of competitiveness to the fest, Clash of Bands and ANTARANG – The Fashion Show being the attraction.

The technical events show why IITs are considered standard in the field of technology. Aeromodelling events which make all gaze the gliders gliding in the open sky. IC car, homemade cars, all terrain vehicle are star attractions. Electronics events where you see participants fiddle with circuits. The main attraction of the technical arena is the ‘ROBO WARS’ where the intense fight between two bots is a sight to die for. The programming and web designing events with best coders showing their talents at the complex coding, deciphering, playing artificial intelligence games and even hacking and cloning the websites.

Special workshops by Mozilla, SAE and various other institutions are organized which provide as a learning tool for tech geeks. The TECHEXPO on the other hand is a great technical carnival with companies like DRDO, TATA and BOSCH setting up their labs and displaying their new innovations in their respective fields. Besides these cultural, technical events are the informals like “Breakthrough” and others which are the fun elements in the fest and also the business events. IGNUS is a classical tale written from the ink of great technical, cultural and educational enthusiasts.

Varchas - Inter-College Sports Fest

“Varchas” is the annual sports fest of IIT Jodhpur. Varchas celebrates the spirit of sportsmanship and serves as a platform to showcase the countless hours of perspiration put in by teams to achieve excellence in their sport. Competitions are held in the fields of Basketball, Football, Volleyball, Cricket, Tennis, Squash, Table Tennis, Athletics, Badminton, Carom and Chess during the sports festival. Competitions are held at national level stadiums such as the Barkatullah Khan stadium and Gaushala Maidan which are a testimony to the success of the fest. More than 900 participants from all over the country participated in the fest this year.

This year “Varchas” witnessed a performance by the upcoming band “Aagman” which has become a sensation among the youth. Along with that the athletes participated whole heartedly in poker nights and other recreational informal events. The students of IIT Jodhpur also organised a first of its kind Mini-Marathon in the streets of Jodhpur and worked for the noble cause of Women Empowerment under the banner-“SOCH”.

Spandan - Intra-College Cultural Festival

Spandan, the intra college cultural fest is one of the most exciting fest of the college. Being the first fest of the year, the enthusiasm, passion, excitement and the struggle to win the championship is just splendid. 3 days of Spandan the campus is awake all night preparing for drama, dance, singing, literature, photography events, informals, fashion show and many more events. Not a single person is left out from taking part in some or other event. It’s a platform for all to showcase their cultural talent. The fest has always had exciting events planned by various clubs.

Spandan is a great cultural fiesta with all rocking the stage with dance, singing, playing games, solving puzzles, debating, enacting, walking down the ramp and living every moment round the clock.

Nimble - Intra-College Techfest

Nimble- Intra collegiate technical fest of IITJ provides students a platform to pursue their zeal towards technology and to achieve new, unexplored zeniths in the field of science. The fest conducted by the technological council has something for everyone to look forward to with all embracing competitions, workshops and lectures. Various clubs organize different competitions in which students from all the years participate with seniors guiding the juniors while competing with each other.

Prominent Events include Balsa Glider, Water Rocket, and Hovercraft by Aero modelling Club; Tricycle by automobile Club; Digital Clock by electronics club; Dottobot by Programming Club; Web Page development by Web Designing Club and many others. One of the main motive of organizing this fest is to give freshers a feel of the technological culture of IITs. Nimble is a great technical carousel where tech geeks come together to create an aura of applied sciences.

Kalakriti - Designers Creaters & Innovators

The M.A.D. Council’s very own Intra-Collegiate ‘Art & Design’ Festival is all for fun, fascination and enjoyment. Hosted by the council in March, ‘Kalakriti’ is a mixed bag of fun-filled competitions, interesting workshops and back-to-back movie screenings which is surely an unmatched way to have a relaxing weekend. “Because everything you can imagine is real!” – These lines wake up the dormant talent amongst the student and the output is sudden burst of colors. With a multitude of events ranging from Fine Arts and Clay Modelling to Photography and Video Editing, ‘Kalakriti’ witnesses huge participation from designers and non-designers alike. Events like ‘Scribble Day’ bring together the entire final year batches to which all the other students bid adieu and leave a farewell message or graffiti on a common piece of cloth given to them. A great way to bring smile on everyone’s face, ‘Kalakriti’ succeeds in spreading joy, unity and togetherness in the small tight-knit community of ours.

Sports GC - The General Championship

Promoting the spirit of competitiveness while preserving that of sportsmanship, Sports General Championship is organized by Sports Council to give Sports enthusiasts a chance to pursue their passion for sports meanwhile competing for the prestigious General Championship Trophy.

Matches are organised with a slight desi tadka to the rules, for instance Gully Cricket, Street Football and Single Court Basketball. Other prominent sports include indoor games like Chess, Carrom, Badminton, Table Tennis, Squash, Swimming and outdoor games like Volleyball. Fullfledged dedication from participants and organisers makes Sports GC one of the liveliest events of the year.
